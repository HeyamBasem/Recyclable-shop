Our Story

The pollution is the main problem that our world suffers from, every single change makes a difference here, one of the greatest solutions that people do not give a lot of attention is recycling. We can all make a difference by practicing proper recycling.

Why should we recycle? It’s a fairly simple concept. When you transform something old into something new, it benefits the environment in a number of ways. Materials and natural resources aren’t wasted. Energy is saved during the manufacturing process. There’s less waste going to landfills. Not to mention, it helps keep wildlife safe. As they said, “Recyclables are collected locally, but impacted globally.”

The big deal question is why don’t people put much effort into saving their world? Do they need something to remind them? maybe feature in their phones or in the applications they used!

From here the idea of EENScycle has come, we wanted to help the world in solving this big problem in a way that benefits all parties. Using technology was the best solution so we started building a website that will make things easier.

Our Mission

We aim to raise awareness about recycling and reusing. We will encourage people to do it with the help of online shopping companies. We want to make it a part of their lifestyle.

What services EENScycle will offer?

For online bought stuffs

At the first step we will contract with online shopping companies and encourage their sellers to add recycling details, including the packaging (papers, cartons, cork, etc.., to the description of the product.

Every product the customer buy will be visible with its recycling details in the app for the them only. When the customer wants to get rid from the product for any reason, by one click they can make the product available for recycling or reusing.
Then:
If it was offered for recycling, then recycling companies contracted with us can view the details and if it’s suitable for them they can come and get it to be recycled and give it a new life.
Or:
If it was offered for reusing it will be available for other customers to get it and make use of it.

For other stuffs

Any other stuff people have can be offered for recycling and reusing in our website. That includes eggs cartons, plastic bottles and cups, boxes, etc…
